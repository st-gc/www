RULES!

Don't be a Dick
We want to be a fun group of people to play with, and not a group of people that just insults each other. Keep this in mind while you are in the TeamSpeak.
Spread the word
We want everyone to have a chance to play games with us. If you've got friends that you play games with, that might be interested, get them involved!
No Racism and no Politics (Zero Tolerance)
This one is pretty self explanatory. If you want to participate in either of these, do it privately.
Be helpful
We want this group to be a very inviting group, so when you see new people around, help them out, be nice to them. If there are issues, talk to a moderator.
Have fun
Last but not least, we want everyone here to have fun, no exceptions!
