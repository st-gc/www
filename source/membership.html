Becoming a Formal Member of Seductive Turtle Gaming Community

STGC is a fairly open community, and it doesn't take much to get a formal invitation to the group, so if you are interested, please keep reading!

You DO NOT need to be a member to play on our servers, or to even rep a (non-member) clan tag while, you play. However, being formally invited to STGC allows you a few cool benefits, like:

  • Reserved Slots on all servers, and Teamspeak.
  • Special ranks on all servers
  • More will appear as we come up with them.

The quickest way to get invited is to play with us a bunch. It definitely wouldnt help to invite your friends to come play as well. As for Moderator and Admin ranks, it may take a while to get there, but if the leadership in STGC thinks you are mature enough, you may just get promoted through those ranks as well.
